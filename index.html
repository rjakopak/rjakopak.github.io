Rhiannon Jakopak



My name is Rhiannon Jakopak, and I am an outreach coordinator and research scientist with the Monteith Shop in the Haub School of Environment and Natural Resources at the University of Wyoming. I enjoy thinking about mammal ecology (especially behavior!), how people across stakeholder groups value wildlife, and how to use science to influence law and policy decisions.

Both for work and play, I am especially happy when I get to spend time outside. Whether I am walking around with my eyes glued to the forest floor in search of fresh deer poop or taking my dogs for a long walk around the "city" of Laramie, I love being outside.

This deep love of being outside makes me want to share how special outside is with everyone that I can! I took the picture to the left after talking to 3rd graders in the Pacific Northwest through Skype a Scientist! I am fortunate that half--yes, half!-- of my work is devoted to science communication. Right now I am mostly focused on K12 engagement and curriculum development.
